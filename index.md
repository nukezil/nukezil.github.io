<h2 style="margin: 60px 0px 10px;">About Me</h2>

I received my Ph.D. and B.Sc. degrees from [School of Computer Science, Nanjing University](http://cs.nju.edu.cn) in 2026 and 2020, respectively. I was extremely fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Yinghuan Shi](https://cs.nju.edu.cn/shiyh/index.htm) and was a member of the [Reasoning and Learning (R&L) Research Group](https://cs.nju.edu.cn/rl/) led by [Prof. Yang Gao](https://cs.nju.edu.cn/gaoyang/).

Starting from April 2026, I will join [Nanjing Normal University](https://ceai.njnu.edu.cn/) as a Lecturer, fortunately working with [Prof. Wanqi Yang](https://wendy-lab-nnu.github.io/).
